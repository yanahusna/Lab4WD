Red Floater

Red Floater is a beautiful floating aquatic plant known for its striking red to purple leaves. It is often used to add color and texture to aquariums or ponds.

Care Requirements:

  • Light: Medium to high light (at least 8-12 hours per day).
  • Temperature: 20-28°C (68-82°F).
  • pH Level: 6.0 to 7.5.
  • Fertilization: Use a liquid fertilizer every two weeks for best growth.

Hornwort

Hornwort is a hardy, submerged plant that provides excellent cover and oxygenation for your aquatic environment.

Price: RM 10.00

Care Requirements:

  • Light: Low to moderate light.
  • Temperature: 15-28°C (59-82°F).
  • pH Level: 6.0 to 7.5.
  • Fertilization: Minimal, but adding CO2 can enhance growth.

Java Moss

Java Moss is a versatile moss perfect for creating a natural look in your aquarium while offering shelter for fish and shrimp.

Care Requirements:

  • Light: Low to moderate light.
  • Temperature: 20-28°C (68-82°F).
  • pH Level: 5.0 to 8.0.
  • Fertilization: Fertilization is not strictly necessary, but liquid fertilizers can promote growth.
